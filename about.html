aBout us

The mind and heart behind Bobs & Bangs is Tilda and Mathias. Tilda has been a hair stylist since 2014 who specilizes in cutting, styling and colouring. Mathias has been working as a barber since 2012 but is also passionate about skincare. Tilda and Mathias met at a sci-fi convention in Stockholm and fell in love.

In the late spring of 2019, they decided that their next career path was to open their own hair salon/barbershop - together! They both strive to make this a place where people from all walks of life can synonimize the services of Bobs & Bangs with personal, attentive, professional and of the highest quality.

Still not convinced to try out their services? Check out the customer reviews on the Home page and their socials at the bottom of the page. Tilda and Mathias have big plans for the future of Bobs & Bangs and you are all invited to follow their journey!
